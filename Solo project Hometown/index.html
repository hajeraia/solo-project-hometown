Welcome to Riyadh city

Four activities to visit Riyadh

Kingdom Tower

An elegant center and hall for celebrations, office floors, office suites, and luxurious residential apartments, the Four Seasons Hotel and international restaurants.

Masmak Palace

Al-Masmak was the scene of the battle for the conquest of Riyadh, in which Abdulaziz Al Saud regained the city of Riyadh, and the traces of the famous battle are still present at the door of the palace to the present day. It now contains a museum dedicated to the unification of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Al Faisaliyah Tower

It consists of an office tower with 30 floors and rises to the top in a hierarchical shape with curved edges topped with a glass ball, a banquet hall, a commercial market consisting of three floors, and a ground floor that includes 150 shops.

Historic Diriyah

Al-Diriyah represents a prominent national symbol in the history of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, it was its capital. And it continued until Turki bin Abdullah chose Riyadh as a new seat of government.
